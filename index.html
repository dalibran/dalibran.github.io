Services

Discover

So you've got an idea, now what? I use the most up-to-date product management techniques to mitigate the risk inherent in product development. By interviewing potential customers prior to development, we can better understand the problem that our product attempts to solve.

Design

Leverage the best user experience techniques to craft products that users love. By working closely with UX designers, I can iteratively manage the design process. The end result is aesthetically pleasing, intuitive product.

Plan

Feel secure in your investment by playing an integral role in the product planning process. Features are prioritized by a combination of level of effort and customer importance. Roadmaps keep stakeholders up-to-date and aligned regarding progress at each stage of development.

Manage

As a Certified ScrumMaster, I can manage the development phase of your project. I'll define milestones, protect against scope creep, and proactively address any issues that hinder product development.

Optimize

After product launch, I'll optimize your product further by analyzing user behavior and data. New product features are informed by the market, thereby driving growth and increasing your chances of user adoption.

Educate

Turn your workforce into competant product professionals. Periodic workshops educate stakeholders on the product development process, resulting in actionable feedback, fewer delays, and better products.
